## Automation Notes

- The raw recording captured the business flow, but it needs cleanup before it can be executed as-is.
- `recording.spec.js` references `page` before creating it, then declares `const page = await context.newPage();` near the end. A runnable spec should create the page immediately after the browser context is created.
- The recording did not capture username/password entry. It captured the auth redirect and two **Continue** clicks before entering the application.
- Several selectors are generated ExtJS IDs, such as `#iBEBusObjType-8815-trigger-picker`, `#button-1109`, and `#gridview-1595`. These should be replaced with stable labels, roles, helper methods, or scoped locators before this becomes a maintained automation test.
